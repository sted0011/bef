My right brain flourishes: I'm a designer, visual crafter, and idea connector. I like to make turkeys dance on social media. Bring these together and you have a powerhouse designer who can anticipate outcomes, solve problems, and digitally implement (translation: I can code too).

Creative. Strategic. Smart.

I was trained in web design at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, studied Journalism, Advertising, and Public Relations at the University of Minnesota, and mastered law at The George Washington University. In other words, I am a curious creative who loves the Oxford comma.{% endif %}
